Profile Notes

Michel Tusche is a pony character I created as a personal OC. He's a fluffy white pony with a dark blue mane, tail and eyes. His cutie mark is a splat of paint. He really likes drawing his friends, drinking hot chocolate, and napping. He has stitching on his body because he's actually a plush toy brought to life.

If you want to draw Michel Tusche, please refer to the character rules below and the reference images in the next section.

Character rules

Michel Tusche is a personal OC, so there are certain guidelines I ask people to follow when drawing him. If you want to draw him, please follow these rules:

  • Don't depict my character with any sort of injuries, gore or violent content.

    • - My character is meant to be cute and friendly. Portraying him in any emotionally scaring way is not allowed.

  • No gross/unflattering depictions or fetish art.

    • - I don't allow fetish content with my character.

  • Don't use my character to promote any product, service, politics, etc. without permission.

    • - My character is not your mascot. I love to be included in things I enjoy, like events and promotions, but please don't do it without my permission.

  • No art that promotes hate, discrimination, or harmful stereotypes.

    • - My character is meant to be a positive presence. Please don't use him in any way that promotes negativity or harm.

  • No art depicting my character having romantical or sexual interactions with other characters.

    • - My character is happily married and faithful to his partner.
