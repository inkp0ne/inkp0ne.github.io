Terms

  • 50% upfront, 50% after sketch approval.
  • No tight deadlines.
  • Payment via PayPal or Ko-fi.
  • Copyright retained by artist, with commercial use negotiable.
  • Typical turnaround is 1-3 weeks depending on complexity.
  • Requesting extra variants is subject to additional fees.
  • No AI training or NFT usage permitted.
  • Banned commission themes will be rejected without discussion.
